# Onboarding Screens Content

This file contains the text for the introduction screens of Nmail. 

## Screen 1: Welcome
*   **Title:** Welcome to Nmail
*   **Description:** Discover a decentralized email experience that puts you in control. A new way to communicate, centered around you.
*   **Icon:** `App Logo`

## Screen 2: Decentralization
*   **Title:** A Network Without Masters
*   **Description:** Your messages flow through a global network of independent servers. No single company owns your inbox.
*   **Icon:** `Icons.hub_outlined`

## Screen 3: Sovereignty & Choice
*   **Title:** Freedom of Choice
*   **Description:** You are never locked into a single provider. Switch bridges or relays at any time without ever losing your identity or contacts.
*   **Icon:** `Icons.swap_horiz`

## Screen 4: Identity
*   **Title:** One Identity for Everything
*   **Description:** Use your account to send emails, follow profiles, or use other apps. It's one permanent identity that works across many different applications.
*   **Icon:** `Icons.apps_outlined`

## Screen 5: Future-Proof Security
*   **Title:** Built for the Future
*   **Description:** Benefit from a modern architecture designed for privacy. Nmail helps you transition smoothly to a more secure and resilient way of communicating.
*   **Icon:** `Icons.auto_awesome`
